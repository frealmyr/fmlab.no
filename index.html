Here i will post tutorials, tips and tricks for homelabs and cloud infrastructure whenever i feel a writer's itch.

Current hardware

I use a Intel NUC7i5 with two WD MyBook V2 USB3.0 8TB HDDs.

This NUC is my homeserver, HTPC and moonlight gamestreaming client which is connected to my 4K TV. It handles most of the things i throw at it, the only real limitation i have encountered are demanding game servers.
